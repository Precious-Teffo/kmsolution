Digital Self-Protection

Everyday People Deserve to Feel Safe Online

You don't need to be a tech expert to protect yourself in the digital world. We teach ordinary individuals practical, easy-to-understand skills that guard your privacy, your identity, and your life online — against real threats that affect real people every day.

🔐

Personal Online Safety & Privacy

Learn how to protect your personal information, secure your email and accounts with strong passwords and two-factor authentication, and understand what data apps and websites collect about you — and how to limit it.

🎣

Protecting Yourself Against Hackers & Scams

Hackers and scammers target ordinary people every day. We teach you how to recognise phishing emails, fake SMS messages, phone scams, and fraudulent websites — so you never fall victim to attacks designed to steal your money or identity.

📱

Safe Internet & Social Media Use

From managing your Facebook privacy settings to using public Wi-Fi safely, knowing what not to share online, and spotting fake profiles and misinformation — we give you the confidence to navigate the internet without fear.

🛡️

Securing Your Devices & Digital Life

Your phone, laptop, and smart devices are gateways to your entire life. Learn the simple daily habits — software updates, secure Wi-Fi, safe app downloads, and data backups — that keep everything you own and everyone you love protected.
